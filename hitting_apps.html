Custom Hitter Comparison

Ever seen those Stathead comparisons on Social Media? Frustrated that it doesn't include defense, baserunning, and Statcast metrics? You can fully customize your own graphics, choose from presets, and even compare historical players, all over any span of years.

Custom Hitter Savant Page

You can also customize a hitter's Baseball Savant page and include or exclude whichever stats you prefer. Pages from years prior to Statcast can also be created with non-Statcast stats, all the way back to the 1800s. You can automatically list stats from greatest to least, or vice versa, too.

Custom Team Hitting Savant Page

Curious which players from one team led in certain stats? You can sort by minimum plate appearances and see a team's best hitters in any statistics for any year, savant page style.

Custom Hitter Leaderboard

You can easily display the ten best or worst hitters in any given stat with this app. For rate stats, a minimum PA threshold can be created and displayed at the top. If a player's headshot doesn't load, enter their MLB ID (found in their MLB profile url) to load their picture.
