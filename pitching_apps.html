Custom Pitcher Comparison

Want to compare Tarik Skubal and Paul Skenes with advanced stats such as xERA, FIP, and Barrel%? Now you can. A plethora of statistics are available to compare and contrast up to four pitchers at a time. Presets which automatically choose all stats a certain pitcher leads in are also available, for any agenda pushers.

Custom Pitcher Savant Page

This app allows you to customize any pitcher's savant page with Statcast stats, but also WAR, FIP, IP, and many others. Percentiles are based on pitchers with at least 40 IP that season, same as on Baseball Savant. Auto-sorting stats by percentile are also available.

Custom Team Pitching Savant Page

You can create a savant page of the best pitchers from any team in any stat. Percentiles are the same as Baseball Savant: pitchers with at least 40 IP that season.

Custom Pitcher Leaderboard

Interested in displaying the 10 best or worst pitchers in any statistic in any year? Choose a minimum IP, year, and screenshot to save your custom made graphic. Multi-year spans will take longer to load and may exclude those who didn't reach the min IP threshold (which saves loading times) for any single year.

Year-over-Year Pitcher Risers & Fallers

Ever wondered who the breakout pitchers were over the last year? Or which aces fell off? Now you can see the top 10 improvers and decliners in any pitching stat you wish. The min PA filter applies to both years, and players can be selected based on their ending team and position.
