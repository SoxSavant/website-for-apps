Custom Pitcher Comparison

Want to compare Tarik Skubal and Paul Skenes with advanced stats such as xERA, FIP, and Barrel%? Now you can. A plethora of statistics are available to compare and contrast up to four pitchers at a time. Presets which automatically choose all stats a certain pitcher leads in are also available, for any agenda pushers.

Custom Pitcher Savant Page

This app allows you to customize any pitcher's savant page with Statcast stats, but also WAR, FIP, IP, and many others. Percentiles are based on pitchers with at least 40 IP that season, same as on Baseball Savant. Auto-sorting stats by percentile are also available.
