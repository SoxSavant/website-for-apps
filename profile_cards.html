MLB Hitter Profile Card

Ever needed a quick snapshot of a hitter's year? Here you can generate a profile card which includes stats such as AVG, OPS, HR, and RBI, as well as the player's age and season year. The color of the headshot border and team name are color coded with the team's primary color too.

MLB Pitcher Profile Card

Creation of a pitcher's profile card from any year is also possible. Display ERA, IP, W-L and other common stats with the click of a button. Data before 1900 may be inaccurate.
