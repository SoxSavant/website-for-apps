MLB Hitter Profile Card

Quickly generate a hitter’s profile card for any season. Includes stats like AVG, OPS, HR, RBI, player age, and season year. The headshot border and team name are color-coded to the team’s primary color.

MLB Pitcher Profile Card

Create a pitcher’s profile card for any season. Displays stats such as ERA, IP, W-L, and other main pitching metrics. Note that data before 1900 may be less accurate.
